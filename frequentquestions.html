Frequent Questions

What it is gazpacho?

Gazpacho is a kind of cold soup that is served especially on hot summer days as it helps to hydrate the body thanks to its high percentage of water and mineral salts. This delicious smoothie is made from fresh vegetables such as tomatoes, cucumbers, capsicum, onions and garlic, which are blended with olive oil, vinegar and salt. The mixture is mashed to a smooth texture and served very cold.

Who created it?

Gazpacho originates from the south of Spain (Andalusia) where the day labourers and peasants, in order to make use of the dried bread from the previous days, soaked it in water, added hand-squeezed tomatoes and seasoned it with garlic, oil and pepper.

How many calories are in a glass of gazpacho?

One glass contains between 100 and 150 calories. This means that gazpacho is a low-calorie option and perfect for those looking to maintain their weight or follow a healthy diet.

what are the benefits ?

As it is not subjected to a thermal process and is a raw mixture, its properties are preserved intact at the time of consumption, being rich in vitamins A, C and D, which will help you strengthen your immune system, maintain healthy skin, improve vision and regulate blood clotting.

Is Gazpacho gluten free?

Our homemade gazpacho made with all-natural products is a naturally gluten-free food, as it does not contain any ingredients that contain this protein. This makes it an excellent choice for those who follow a gluten-free diet.
