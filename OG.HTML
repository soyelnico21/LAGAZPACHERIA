What is Gazpacho?

Gazpacho is a kind of cold soup that is served especially on hot summer days as it helps to hydrate the body thanks to its high percentage of water and mineral salts. This delicious smoothie is made from fresh vegetables such as tomatoes, cucumbers, capsicum, onions and garlic, which are blended with olive oil, vinegar and salt. The mixture is mashed to a smooth texture and served very cold.

Our story

Say Hello to Manu and Toni, two mates from Spain carving out their dreams in the sunny land of Australia. Bonded by their love for Spanish cuisine, they often found themselves cooking up memories from home.

After a heartwarming reunion with friends Down Under, the idea struck them: why not share the taste of Spain with their newfound Aussie mates? And so, “Lagazpacheria” was born.

Their mission is simple: to let Australians experience the refreshing delight of authentic gazpacho, one sip at a time.
